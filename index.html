Focus Companion: Enhances Your Productivity

Focus Companion is a Chrome extension designed to help users stay productive by minimizing online distractions. It automatically fetches all open tabs and allows users to categorize them as either Focus or Distracting. A customizable threshold time ensures that distracting tabs are closed once their limit is exceeded, while Focus Mode continuously monitors and auto-closes such tabs until the user deactivates it. The extension also includes a monitoring timer to prevent indefinite blocking.
